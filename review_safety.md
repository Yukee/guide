Critical Issues (fix before publishing)
1. DMT "releases serotonin" — factually wrong (line 57)

"When combined with DMT (which also releases serotonin), the effects can stack."

DMT is a serotonin receptor agonist — it binds to and activates serotonin receptors. It does not release serotonin. Fix: change to "which also activates serotonin receptors."

2. 24-hour triptan washout insufficient for frovatriptan (line 77)
Frovatriptan (Frova) has a half-life of 26-30 hours — 24 hours is not enough. The guide should add an exception: "Most triptans clear within 24 hours, but frovatriptan (Frova) requires at least 5 days."
