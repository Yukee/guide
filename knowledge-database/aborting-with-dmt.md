# Aborting with DMT

This entry covers the dosing and delivery specifics relevant to aborting cluster headache attacks with inhaled DMT. It focuses on how much active substance reaches the user per inhalation across different devices and concentrations.

## Aborting Dose Range

Community reports consistently indicate that very small amounts of vaporized DMT — far below psychedelic thresholds — can abort a cluster headache attack:

- **3 mg vaporized** is cited as sufficient to abort an attack within 3-5 seconds [1][2]
- The effective aborting range appears to be roughly **3-10 mg**, with most users needing only 1-2 puffs from a vape pen [1][2]
- For comparison, a psychedelic threshold is ~10-20 mg and a full breakthrough dose is ~30-50 mg (method-dependent) [3]

## DMT Delivered Per Puff: Vape Cartridges (510-Thread)

### How the math works

A standard 0.5 ml vape cartridge delivers approximately **75-150 puffs** (varies by device, coil, draw duration, and voltage). The DMT content per cartridge depends on the concentration of the liquid:

| Concentration | DMT in 0.5 ml cartridge | Est. mg per puff (short draw) | Est. mg per puff (long draw) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 200 mg/ml | 100 mg | ~0.7-1.3 mg | ~2-4 mg |
| 333 mg/ml (1:3 ratio) | 167 mg | ~1.1-2.2 mg | ~3-7 mg |
| 500 mg/ml (1:2 ratio) | 250 mg | ~1.7-3.3 mg | ~5-10 mg |
| 1000 mg/ml (1:1 ratio) | 500 mg | ~3.3-6.7 mg | ~10-20 mg |

### Key variables

The table above should be understood as rough estimates. Per-puff delivery varies considerably due to:

1. **Draw duration**: A 3-second puff vs. a 10-second pull can differ by 3-4x. One DMT-Nexus user measured ~4 mg DMT per second of inhalation from a concentrated (700 mg/0.3 ml) cartridge [4].
2. **Voltage/wattage**: Higher settings vaporize more liquid per second.
3. **Coil type and condition**: Fresh ceramic coils deliver more consistently than aged or gunked coils.
4. **Inhalation technique**: Deep lung draws absorb more than shallow mouth draws.
5. **Liquid viscosity**: Higher VG ratios wick more slowly, potentially reducing per-puff delivery.

### The 2.7 mg figure

A commonly cited estimate is **2.7 mg per puff** from a 400 mg/0.5 ml cartridge (~800 mg/ml), based on dividing total DMT by ~150 puffs [5][6]. This is a reasonable ballpark for short, casual puffs. Longer, deliberate draws deliver substantially more.

### Practical implication for aborting

At the guide's recommended concentration of 333 mg/ml, **a single moderate puff delivers roughly 2-5 mg of DMT** — likely sufficient to abort most attacks based on community reports. A second puff a minute later covers nearly all remaining cases [1][2]. This means high concentrations (500+ mg/ml) are unnecessary for aborting and introduce clogging risk without therapeutic benefit.

## DMT Delivered Per Hit: Direct-Loading Devices

These methods place DMT crystal/powder directly on a heating element rather than dissolving it in liquid. They are included for completeness but are **not recommended for aborting** — they deliver larger, less controllable doses and are harder to titrate.

### E-mesh

DMT is weighed and placed directly on a stainless steel mesh coil in a box mod. The user fires the mod and inhales the entire dose in one 5-7 second pull.

- Typical loading: 15-30 mg for a full experience [7]
- Efficiency: Near 100% — considered the most efficient consumer method [7][8]
- Breakthrough threshold: 15-25 mg [7]
- Not well-suited for aborting: the minimum practical loading (~5-10 mg) already approaches or exceeds the aborting range, and there is no way to take a partial dose

### Glass Vapor Genie (GVG)

Convection-based: heated air passes over DMT crystals in a ceramic element.

- Typical loading: 15-40 mg [3][8]
- Breakthrough threshold: 30-40 mg
- Less efficient than e-mesh; requires skilled torch technique
- 1-3 inhales per loaded dose

### Oil burner pipe

Direct conduction heating of DMT in a glass bulb.

- Typical loading: 20-30 mg [8]
- Significant waste — residue left as brownish oil
- Least efficient common method
- Breakthrough requires 40-60+ mg due to losses [8]

### Efficiency ranking (community consensus)

1. E-mesh (highest — near-complete vaporization)
2. Glass Vapor Genie (high, technique-dependent)
3. The Machine (moderate)
4. Oil burner pipe (lowest — significant waste)

Source: DMT-Nexus efficiency comparison thread [8]

## Self-Calibration Method

Because per-puff delivery varies so much by device and technique, one practical approach from the DMT-Nexus community is the **weigh-before-and-after method** [4]:

1. Weigh the cartridge on a milligram scale before use
2. Take a timed puff (e.g., 5 seconds)
3. Weigh the cartridge again
4. Weight difference x concentration = mg of DMT delivered

This lets each user determine their specific device's delivery rate. For cluster headache patients, this calibration step could help establish confidence in dosing before relying on the method during an attack.

## Evidence Quality Note

The per-puff delivery estimates in this entry are derived from:

- **Community forum reports** (DMT-Nexus, Reddit r/DMT, Bluelight) — anecdotal but consistent across many independent users
- **Basic arithmetic** applied to known cartridge volumes and puff counts from the broader vaping industry
- **One user-reported measurement** of mg consumed per second of inhalation [4]

No published pharmacokinetic study has measured per-puff DMT delivery from consumer vape devices. The only clinical trial of inhaled DMT (BMND01, phase I) used pharmaceutical-grade equipment with precise metered dosing, not consumer cartridges [9]. The estimates here should be treated as informed approximations, not precise measurements.

The aborting dose figure (3 mg) comes from a single interview subject's self-report published in a non-peer-reviewed source [2]. While consistent with the broader pattern of sub-psychedelic efficacy reported by cluster headache communities, it has not been clinically validated.
