# Vaporization and Preparation of DMT

This entry covers the practical aspects of preparing and using DMT in vape form, including equipment, mixing, and storage. The evidence base is primarily community-sourced (forums, harm reduction sites) rather than academic, reflecting the absence of clinical protocols for inhaled DMT outside of pharmaceutical-grade trials.

## Equipment

### Vape pen basics
DMT can be vaped using a standard, small e-cigarette-style vape pen. These devices are inconspicuous — visually identical to a regular nicotine vape pen [1].

### 510-thread standard
Most vape components use a universal screw-on fitting called "510 thread," making batteries, cartridges, and coils interchangeable across manufacturers [1]. This is the dominant standard in the consumer vaping industry.

### Ceramic vs. metal coils
Community consensus and some technical sources recommend ceramic coils over metal coils. Ceramic coils "heat evenly and minimize combustion, preserving the purity and potency of the vapor, resulting in a smoother inhale with better flavor and fewer harsh byproducts" [1][2]. Metal coils are more prone to uneven heating, which can burn DMT rather than vaporizing it.

### Voltage/temperature settings
DMT vaporizes at approximately 160-180°C (320-356°F) [1].
Community guidance suggests:
- Most users find the right setting "between 2.5-3.5 volts" on variable-voltage batteries [1]
- Lower voltage preserves the compound and produces smoother vapor
- Higher voltage produces denser clouds but risks burning
- "A burnt taste means the temperature is too high and DMT is being destroyed rather than vaporized" [16][17]

### Coil care
Firing a coil while dry can permanently damage the wick material inside it, producing a persistent burnt taste. Always ensure liquid is present before firing [18].

## Vape Juice Composition

### PG and VG
Vape juice (e-liquid) is made from two base ingredients [3][4]:
- **Propylene glycol (PG):** A thin, colorless liquid. The FDA considers PG "generally recognized as safe" for oral consumption [4]. It carries flavor well and produces a "throat hit."
- **Vegetable glycerin (VG):** A thicker, slightly sweet liquid derived from vegetable oils. Produces larger vapor clouds but less throat sensation [4].

Both are widely used in food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic products. However, when heated to high temperatures, PG and VG can break down into formaldehyde and acetaldehyde — hence the importance of proper temperature settings [4].

### Recommended PG/VG ratio
Some community sources prefer a higher PG ratio (70/30 PG/VG or even pure PG) because "DMT freebase crystals dissolve easily and cleanly into PG" and a PG-dominant blend "maximizing dissolution and minimizing the chance of the DMT crystallizing inside the tank" [5][6].

## Mixing DMT Vape Liquid

### Concentration ratio
A common starting ratio is 1:3 (1 g DMT per 3 ml vape juice), which is approximately 333 mg/ml.
Community sources indicate that concentrations below 200 mg/ml are stable and unlikely to recrystallize, while concentrations above 250 mg/ml have higher recrystallization risk [5][6].
However, some people report 2DMT:1PG:1VG working fine, see [this thread](https://www.dmt-nexus.me/forum/default.aspx?g=posts&t=91362).
Higher concentrations (up to 1:1 or 1000 mg/ml) are used for breakthrough-dose vaping but are more prone to clogging and are unnecessary for aborting doses — community reports indicate that as little as 3 mg of vaporized DMT can abort a cluster headache attack within seconds [9][10], so even modest concentrations deliver far more than needed per puff.

### Dissolution methods

DMT freebase crystals do not dissolve instantly into PG/VG at room temperature. Several community methods exist. The following table summarizes the options found across DMT-Nexus, Bluelight, and Reddit, ranked by simplicity:

| Method | Simplicity | Reliability | Equipment needed |
|--------|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| Hot water bath ("shot glass method") | ★★★★★ | High | Glass vial, hot water, stirring tool |
| Room-temperature PG dissolution | ★★★★★ | Moderate (slow, low-conc only) | Glass vial only |
| Magnetic stirrer + hotplate | ★★★ | High | Stir plate (~$20-50) |
| Salt conversion (citrate/fumarate) | ★★ | High | Acid, scale, extra chemistry |
| Commercial PEG-based liquidizer | ★★★ | Moderate | Commercial product |
| Shake-and-heat directly in cart | ★★★★ | Low | Just the cartridge |

The two simplest and most reliable methods are described below. In all cases, **use glass containers only**.
DMT is oxidation-sensitive [15], and glass is a much better barrier to oxygen and moisture than typical plastic bottles.

#### Hot water bath (recommended)

The community standard, described in virtually every guide across DMT-Nexus, Bluelight, and Reddit [2][5][11][12]:

1. Add the weighed DMT powder and PG (or PG/VG blend) to a small glass container — a shot glass or glass vial works well.
2. Place the container in a cup or small pot of warm water, approximately 50-70°C (120-160°F). The water should be warm but not boiling — "very warm bath water" temperature [5][11].
3. Stir continuously with a glass rod, metal tool, or the back of a syringe plunger until the liquid turns completely clear and golden, with no visible crystals remaining.
4. At proper temperature, dissolution typically takes under a minute [11][12].

This method works because heat temporarily increases the solubility of DMT in PG and lowers the viscosity of the liquid, allowing crystals to break down rapidly. Stirring ensures even distribution.

Some users warm the glass first (before adding DMT) as a precaution: if the glass cracks from thermal stress, the material isn't lost [11].

#### Room-temperature dissolution (no-heat alternative)

For those who prefer to avoid heat entirely [13]:

1. Add DMT powder and PG to a sealed glass vial.
2. Stir or shake periodically (every 30-60 minutes).
3. Allow to sit for several hours. Some reports describe full dissolution within a few hours; others take up to 2-3 days, depending on concentration and purity.

This method only works reliably at lower concentrations — roughly ≤150-200 mg/ml. At higher concentrations, heat is needed to achieve full dissolution [13].

#### Other methods

A **magnetic stirrer with hotplate** is a more controlled, lab-style variant of the water bath [11]. It produces faster, more consistent results but requires equipment (~$20-50) that most people won't have. More advanced approaches exist (salt conversion to citrate/fumarate, commercial PEG-based liquidizers) but add complexity and introduce their own safety concerns — they are not recommended for this use case.

#### Troubleshooting dissolution

If DMT does not dissolve despite proper method and temperature, the most common causes are [14]:
- **Impure or polymeric DMT:** Yellow or orange material may contain DMT polymers or plant fats with lower solubility. A re-purification step (mini acid-base wash or re-x) usually resolves this.
- **Wrong form:** DMT salts (fumarate, hydrochloride) behave differently from freebase in PG. Confirm the material is freebase.
- **Too much VG:** DMT dissolves primarily in PG, not VG. Mixes with more than ~30% VG may leave undissolved material.

#### Volume displacement note

When DMT powder dissolves into PG, it adds volume — 1 g of DMT plus 1 ml of PG does not yield 1 ml of solution. The resulting volume is roughly 1.5-2 ml [12]. This matters when calculating how much liquid to prepare for a given cartridge size.

### Density approximation
Vape juice has approximately the same density as water, so for practical purposes, 3 grams of vape juice ≈ 3 ml. (PG density: 1.036 g/ml; VG density: 1.261 g/ml; a 50/50 blend is ~1.15 g/ml — close enough to water for rough measurement.)

### Clogging
Undissolved DMT crystals or recrystallized DMT will clog the small intake holes in the cartridge's coil assembly. Ensuring complete dissolution and maintaining appropriate storage temperature prevents this [5].

### Freebase requirement
Only DMT freebase should be used for vaporization. Salt forms (fumarate, hydrochloride) do not vaporize efficiently — they tend to decompose at vaporization temperatures rather than cleanly transitioning to vapor [7]. See [pharmacology-and-mechanism.md](pharmacology-and-mechanism.md) for details on freebase vs. salt chemistry.

## Handling

### Skin contact
DMT powder does not produce psychoactive effects through skin contact. N,N-dimethyltryptamine is not efficiently absorbed through intact skin — specialized transdermal formulations with permeation enhancers are required to achieve meaningful absorption [19][20]. Standard powder-handling precautions (clean workspace, wash hands afterward) are sufficient. This is supported by its molecular properties — while lipophilic enough to cross mucous membranes, passive dermal absorption is minimal.

## Storage and Stability

### Temperature
Room temperature storage (18-25°C / 65-77°F) is ideal. Cold environments cause DMT to recrystallize inside the liquid, potentially clogging the cartridge [5][21].

### Shelf life
DMT freebase is relatively stable when protected from light, heat, and oxygen [22][23]. Mixed DMT vape liquid is generally reported to remain effective for at least several weeks at room temperature, though degradation is faster in solution than in crystalline form [22][23]. Long-term storage is best in an airtight, opaque, glass container — in crystalline form if possible.

### Clearing clogs
If a cartridge clogs (usually from recrystallization in cold conditions), warming it gently — such as holding it in cupped hands or under your armpit for a minute — can re-dissolve the crystals and restore flow [1][21]. More stubborn clogs may require a brief warm water bath.

## Evidence Quality Note

The information in this entry draws primarily from harm reduction forums (DMT-Nexus, Bluelight, Reddit), vaping community guides, and cluster headache patient communities. While consistent across multiple independent sources, this reflects community consensus rather than controlled research. The only clinical trial of inhaled DMT (BMND01, phase I) used pharmaceutical-grade equipment and protocols not directly comparable to consumer vape devices [8].
